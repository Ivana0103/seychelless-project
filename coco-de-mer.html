The Coco de Mer:
Seychelles' Natural Wonder

Introduction

The Coco de Mer is one of the most famous and unique plants in the world.
Native to the Seychelles, this remarkable species is known for its large, distinctive seeds and its role in Seychelles' biodiversity.

Unique Features

The Coco de Mer palm tree produces the largest seed in the plant kingdom, and its fruit can weigh up to 30 kilograms. This rare species is a symbol of Seychelles, treasured for both its beauty and its mysterious history.

Rare and Majestic

Conservation Efforts

Due to its unique nature, the Coco de Mer is protected in Seychelles. Conservation efforts have been implemented to preserve this ancient species and ensure its survival for future generations.
